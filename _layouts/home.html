Biography

I am currently a first year Ph.D. Student at the Department of Computer Science, George Mason University. I am advised by Dr. Bo Han and working closely with Dr. Songqing Chen. Prior to that, I got my B.S. degree in computer science in 2021. My name in Cantonese is Yuichi Shing.

My research interest lies in social VR (Metaverse), deep learning, federated learning, and network measurement. I am currently focusing on the security and privacy issues in the social VR from the system perspective, such as user authentication, harassment, and cyberbullying. I won the best paper award of Metabuild@IEEE VR '22.
